Upgrade

The upgrade page allows you to become a featured user. Becoming a featured user will enable you to be seen by every single user that logs into JBTUS. This will help you to receive more messages and gives you a higher chance at finding what you're looking for.

Gay, Straight, and Bisexual Pages

JBT requests as little information as possible from you when you sign up. The information that we request from you is used to find people of similar interest. The categories that we use are Gay, Straight, or Bisexual. These categories are based only on gender and preference. If you would like to be in a different category, change your gender or preference. After you sign up we place you at the top of whatever category that you fall into. As time passes, you will be pushed back by newer users that join JBTUS. If you would like to continue being seen by everyone, we recommend upgrading your account every once in a while to remain in the spotlight.
